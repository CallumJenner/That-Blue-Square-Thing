That Blue Square Thing

Looking for Doddle homework tasks that have been "lost"? All my Doddle homework, as well as advice on how to solve the problem in Doddle, are on my Doddle page

That Blue Square Thing deals mainly with Applied ICT and Computing stuff these days. In particular, there's material supporting the CiDA and DiDA qualifications, and the Edexcel (2013) Computer Science GCSE. There some old Applied ICT as well.

There's still a bit of geography lurking around here, but I stopped teaching that stuff a while back and the old AQA Spec C is now gone. I've left the revision stuff up though and have even been trying to put some of my old teaching resources up as well. If they're useful for anyone then that's fine. You might be looking for some mock exam mark sheets or controlled assessment timekeepers. You'll find them on the Applied ICT Unit 10 page.

E-Mail:

You can send me E-mail at bluesquarething@gmail.com.
If I teach you then you should probably use my school e-mail address. You can find it on the school e-mail system.
Please make sure that you include a sensible subject line for your e-mail to help me work out what you're writing to me about. If your e-mail address isn't going to tell me who you are then you might want to include your name as well.
Make sure that you check with me first before sending attachments. If I don't expect an attachment I always delete it.
